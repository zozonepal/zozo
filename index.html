Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I place an order on Zojo Nepal?
To place an order, select your desired item, choose your variant color, and add it to your cart. Click on your shopping cart, choose your payment method, click "Proceed to Checkout", and sign in with your Google Account to safely fill out your shipping information.
What payment methods do you support?
We officially support Cash on Delivery (COD) for flexible physical transactions, alongside instant mobile digital wallet payouts via eSewa QR code routing and Khalti QR code transfer systems.
